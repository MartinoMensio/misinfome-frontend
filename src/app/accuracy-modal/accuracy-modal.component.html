Accuracy

Errors could emerge in MisinfoMe's analysis and credibility scoring of tweets. One source of errors is the ClaimReview annotations that fact-checkers publish, which can be incomplete (e.g. pointing to the author of the misinforming link but not the actual link), imprecise (e.g. pointing to a Facebook group instead of a particular post on that group), or misplaced (e.g. placing the misinformation URL in the wrong ClaimReview attribute).

MisinfoMe is a research project, and in continuous development to improve its accuracy, scope, and user interface. Please contact us if you spot any issues and we'll try our best to fix them.
